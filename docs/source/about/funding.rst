Funding
=======

We are committed to developing high-quality, performant software for solving complex vehicle routing problems.
The value of such software is clear: PyVRP is used all over the world by many companies, in many different logistics settings.
However, maintaining and expanding the software is nontrivial and expensive, and we could use your help to ensure the project remains in a healthy state.

If you benefit from our work, please consider supporting us in one of the following ways:

* Sponsoring the project on `GitHub <https://github.com/sponsors/PyVRP>`_.
* Funding the development of new features.
  Please contact us at `info@routinglab.tech <mailto:info@routinglab.tech>`_ if you wish to fund a specific feature.
* Using `RoutingLab <https://routinglab.tech/>`_'s products or services.

.. important::

   Your support is a tremendous help towards new feature development and faster bug fixes.
